Wii

Nintendo released the Wii in 2006 and sold iterations of it until 2017. It is the fourth best selling home console ever.

Part of its claim to fame is in its unique remote controls. The remotes capture both motion and point controls. This allowed for many novel games on the Wii system.

The Wii came with Wii Sports, which is the best selling console game ever with 82 million copies worldwide. It combined improved disc graphics with new remote controls. The next home console to follow the Wii was the Wii U, but nobody liked it.
