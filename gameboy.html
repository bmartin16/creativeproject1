Gameboy

The release of the Gameboy shocked the market. It was originally an 8 bit system that took 4 AA batteries and large cartridges.

The Gameboy and Gameboy Advance sold a total of 200 million units from 1989-2010. It is the best selling console, handheld or not, of all time.

Subsequent versions included color, a backlight, and a clamshell design. The Gameboy set Nintendo up nicely for their next lineup of handhelds, with the DS selling over a hundred million units as well.
