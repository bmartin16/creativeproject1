Switch

The Nintendo Switch was released in 2017 and is Nintendo’s most current gaming console. Unlike the Wii or N64, the Switch is a hybrid console.

The Switch has two Joy-Cons that function as detachable controllers. They have motion controls similar to that of Wii controllers.

Cartridges are supported on the switch similar to those used in Gameboys and the N64. Despite that, many games are digitally downloaded onto the device itself. The Switch is hugely successful and has sold almost 90 million units. It is on track to be Nintendo’s best selling console.
