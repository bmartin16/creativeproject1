Nintendo 64

The Nintendo 64 was a breakthrough in Nintendoâ€™s lineup of home consoles. It delivered novel games with stunning 3D graphics that shaped the industry.

The 3D graphics of the N64 were on proper display with the debut of Super Mario 64 at launch. The game featured an expansive free-roaming world with many secrets to discover. It set the bar for fun interactive 3D gaming environments.

Four person multiplayer was another groundbreaking feature. Few competing consoles offered it, and those that did had decent lag.

The N64 used a cartridge system to load games, like its predecessor the Super Nintendo. Though cartridges have minimal storage, they provide blazing fast transfer speeds that take the games to a new level.
